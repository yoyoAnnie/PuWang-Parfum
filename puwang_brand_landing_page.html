Philosophy
We do not create new smells.
We recover real ones.

Every PuWang fragrance begins not in a laboratory but in a memory — cross-referenced against cultural geography, generational recall, and endangered sensory landscapes. The nose that designed it spent months in the field before touching a single molecule. The AI that assisted it was trained on olfactory literature spanning four decades and twelve languages. The result is a scent that does not smell like what is fashionable. It smells like what is true.
